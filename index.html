About me

I'm a Physical Oceanographer scientist at the MetOcean Solutions organization. I have obtained my Oceanographer B.Sc. degree at UERJ and my M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees at IOUSP, being the later in collaboration with SMAST-UMASSD. I'm also an associate professor at Oceanography Graduation program at FAOC-UERJ, where I'm collaborating with the Data Analysis in Physical Oceanography course and supervising one M.Sc. student.
